POST /api/submit

Submit an invite. Requires a key and invite. Invite must be either in the form of https://discord.gg/ID or https://discord.com/invite/ID

        curl --data-raw "{'token': 'your_token_here', 'url': 'your_invite_url_here'}" -H "Content-type: application/json" {{ full_domain }}
